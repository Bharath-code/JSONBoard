The 5 conversion moments — ranked by conversion rate
🔴 1. Link expiry email (highest converter)
Sent 48h before free link expires. Subject: "Your JSONBoard dashboard expires in 2 days".
Body: "Dashboard '[name]' shared [date] expires on [date+7]. Pro keeps it for 90 days — $9/month. One click to upgrade and keep your link alive."

Why it works: the user already has the dashboard. Losing it is loss aversion. Loss aversion converts ~3x better than feature desire.
Est. conversion: 8–15% of active users who get this email
🟠 2. File size rejection (mid-funnel)
User pastes JSON > 1 MB. Instead of a generic error: "This file is 2.3 MB. Free tier supports up to 1 MB. Pro supports 10 MB — upgrade for $9/month." with inline Stripe checkout.
Est. conversion: 6–10% of users who hit the limit
🟣 3. Password protection request
Free user clicks "Protect with password" in share modal. A modal appears: "Password protection is a Pro feature. Upgrade for $9/month to keep your data private." The intent here is HIGH — they care enough to want privacy.
Est. conversion: 5–8% of users who try to set a password
🔵 4. Branded footer click (viral + convert)
Viewer clicks "Made with JSONBoard" on a shared dashboard. They land on homepage with a banner: "You're seeing this because someone shared a dashboard with you. Create yours free — or upgrade to remove the branding."
Est. conversion: 2–4% of footer clicks (but high volume)
🟢 5. 7th-day in-product nudge
On the 7th day after a free user's first dashboard, show an in-product banner: "You've created [N] dashboards. Your earliest one expires in [X] hours. Pro gives you 90-day links, password protection, and 10× larger files — $9/month."
Est. conversion: 3–6% of weekly active users
